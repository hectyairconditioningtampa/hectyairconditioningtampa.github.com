Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services in Tampa.

❓ Do I need to schedule an appointment?
We recommend scheduling to guarantee immediate service, but we also handle urgent cases based on availability at our Tampa shop.
❓ How long does an A/C diagnostic take?
A full diagnostic usually takes between 30 to 60 minutes. We use scanners and electronic leak detectors for precision.
❓ Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?
Yes, all our mechanical and electrical work is covered by a warranty, except for electrical parts which are sold without warranty.
❓ Do you work with all Thermo King brands?
We specialize in Regular and Evolution Thermo King APU units.
❓ Where exactly are you located?
We are strategically located at 10025 US-301, Tampa, FL 33637. This is an easy access point for both trucks and private vehicles.
